About Navjeevan Gramoday Pratishthan

Pioneering Rural and Urban Development Since 1994

Our Story

Navjeevan Gramodaya Prathistan (NGP) is working since 1994 in the field of rural & urban development.

The major thrust areas of organization is Natural resource management through watershed development and water sector for community, women & child development for health & education aspects and livelihood development for deprived section including landless families of society.

In totality, NGP has benefited over more than 65,000 people through different development projects.

Our Vision

To create a sustainable and Homogeneous Society based on the principles of legitimate rights, equality and Justice.

Our Mission

Holistic development of socially and economically deprived, excluded and marginalized sections of the Indian society through capacity building, promoting peoples organizations, managing natural resources and developing.

Our Target Group

Target communities served by Navjeevan Gramoday Pratisthan (NGP) are from the lowest strata of society, especially women, children, the vulnerable & infirm. NGP works for the up-liftment of people limited by a low status in society in the areas of social, economic, domestic, personal and cultural development.
