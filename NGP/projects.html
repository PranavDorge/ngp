Completed Projects

NABARD - IGWDP (Indo German Watershed Development Project) (2004-2012)

Successfully completed four IGWDP projects in Ahmednagar and Hingoli districts. Total area covered: 4513.28 ha with 5196 population.

Community Action for Poverty Alleviation (CAPA) (2002-03)

Constructed check dams and drinking water supply schemes for 4 villages of Ahmednagar district, benefiting 4182 rural people. Funded by WOTR.

Apale Pani (Water supply scheme) (2007-08)

Implemented in 17 villages across Pune and Ahmednagar districts, making drinking water available to over 21,541 people. Awarded as a most crucial project for drinking water.
