Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To create a sustainable and Homogeneous Society based on the principles of legitimate rights, equality and Justice.

Mission

Holistic development of socially and economically deprived, excluded and marginalized sections of the Indian society through capacity building, promoting peoples organizations, managing natural resources and developing.

About NGP

Navjeevan Gramodaya Prathistan (NGP) is working since 1994 in the field of rural & urban development. The major thrust areas of organization is Natural resource management through watershed development and water sector for community, women & child development for health & education aspects and livelihood development for deprived section including landless families of society.

In totality, NGP has benefited over more than 65,000 people through different development projects.

Our Thematic Areas

Child Protection, Development & Human Rights

We are dedicated to creating a safe and nurturing environment for children. Our work focuses on protecting them from harm, ensuring their access to education and health services, and upholding their fundamental human rights to give them a chance at a brighter future.

Health and Sanitation

Promoting community well-being is at the core of our health initiatives. We work to improve access to healthcare services, conduct awareness campaigns on hygiene and sanitation, and implement projects that ensure clean drinking water and proper sanitation facilities for all.

Women Empowerment

We believe that empowering women is key to transforming entire communities. Through the formation of Self-Help Groups (SHGs), skill development programs, and promoting financial independence, we help women become leaders and agents of change in their families and society.

Educational Development

Education is a powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty. We focus on improving access to quality education, reducing dropout rates, and creating a conducive learning environment in schools. Our efforts aim to unlock the potential of every child and young adult.

Natural Resource Management & Environmental Protection

Our watershed development and sustainable agriculture projects are designed to protect and restore the environment. We work with communities to manage natural resources effectively, promote eco-friendly practices, and ensure ecological balance for a sustainable future.
